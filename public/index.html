What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) calculator helps you estimate the monthly repayment for loans based on amount, tenure, and interest rate. EMIWhiz allows you to compare multiple loans side-by-side and optimize repayments.

Features of EMIWhiz

  • Compare home, personal, and car loans
  • Adjust prepayment percentage for savings insights
  • Visualize detailed amortization schedule
  • Download EMI data in CSV format

Frequently Asked Questions

How is EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula: EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N-1], where P is the loan amount, R is the monthly interest rate, and N is the number of payments.

Can I make partial prepayments?

Yes, EMIWhiz allows you to simulate partial prepayments and see how they reduce total interest and EMI duration.
